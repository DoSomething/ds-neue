Staff Retreat

For one week each year, we take the entire staff off-site on Staff Retreat. We spawn innovative new ideas for the organization, bond with one another, and take a step back and evaluate where we are and where we're going.

Failfest

We don't fire because of failures. We build, plan, and design things that may fail on the chance that they might be amazing. We hold an annual "Failfest" where we highlight these failures in front of the whole staff — what went wrong, what went right, and how we learned from it. After working at DoSomething.org for two years, staff members get a special treat — four weeks of sabbatical. Basically, you get a month of paid vacation to volunteer anywhere in the world you want to go. We send our staff out with hopes that they'll come back with new ideas and experiences.
