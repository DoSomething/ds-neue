We're making "social good" cool. Come do something with us.

View Current Openings

A workplace you'll want to spend time in.

We have a beautiful open floorplan office in the Flatiron District where we work together collaboratively. No cubicles.

We don't have offices.

Every few months we put everyone's names into a hat (including the CEO!) and as names are called, each person rushes to claim a new desk around the office.

Thinking Thursdays

If this all sounds hectic, we have Thinking Thursdays, where the entire office is silent so we can focus on getting work done without interruptions.

We're not your mom's not-for-profit.

We're a data-driven organization with a world-class engineering team. If you're interested in crunching massive data or building solutions that scale, you'll have fun here.

We learn from failure.

We don't fire because of failures. We build things that may fail on the chance that they might be amazing. We hold an annual Failfest where we highlight our mistakes and what we learned from them. And yes, you have to wear a pink boa while you present.

We're good sharers.

We write about our engineering and design process on our tech blog, and we maintain open-source code for the community. We also host a monthly "Tech For Good" meetup in our office.

We invest in our best asset — you.

We hire smart, talent people who we trust to start kicking ass on their first day, and still be taking names two years later.

Get outside the box.

For one week each year, we take the entire staff off-site on staff retreat. We spawn innovative new ideas for the organization, take a step back and evaluate where we are and where we're going, and bond with each another with fun games & activities.

Take a break.

After two years at DoSomething, we let you do something amazing — take a break. We give all employees a four-week sabbatical to go volunteer somewhere else, and bring back new ideas and perspectives to us when they're done.




Check out our staff page to learn more about our employees and culture.

Employee Perks

Competetive salary

We know money isn't what drives you, but you need to pay the bills. All of our employees are paid a fair salary (on a not-for-profit scale).

Great benefits

We offer comprehensive medical, dental, vision, life insurance, and 403(b) savings plans.

3 weeks paid vacation

Work may be play, but sometimes you need a break. We get that and give all employees 3 weeks paid vacation plus major holidays.

Discounted Gym membership

We offer a discounted membership at a top-notch gym facility. And if you're not that hardcore, we have our own gym area in the office.

Summer Fridays

We take half-days every Friday during the summer so you can enjoy the great weather.

Made in NYC.

We're located in the Flatiron neighborhood in New York City. Be a part of one of the world's greatest cultural centers.

…not convinced yet? Here's more reasons you'll love working here:

Arcade machine in the lunch room

With 60 classic arcade games & a competetive high-score ladder.

Summer Kickball League

Our interns & staff demolish* other non-profit organizations in kickball.

*actual results may vary.

Summer Spirit Days

Every Summer Friday, you're challenged to come into work in a themed outfit.

Standing Desks

We have communal standing desks located around the office.

Summer Bake-Offs

Needless to say, we eat a lot of brownies during the summer.

The Penguin

Every Wednesday, we award The Penguin to a staff member who kicked ass the past week.

Pre-tax Commuter Plans

Save money on your MetroCard or Metro-North train tickets.

No work on your birthday

Nobody wants to spend their birthday in the office (even one as awesome as ours), so we give you a free vacation day on your birthday.

Bring Your Dog To Work

Bring man's best friend to work! We have a calendar to schedule dog visits.
